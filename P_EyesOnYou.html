Home EYES ON YOU

Empathize with myopic people

Eyes on you is a myopia simulator, an awareness-raising experience that allows people who do not have this condition to put themselves in the shoes of those who do suffer from it.

The player finds himself in a park and has to find the person he is meeting. The problem is that he has forgotten his glasses and this search will not be easy for him. You can select 3 grades of myopia.

You can move throughout the park, where 24 benches will be available where 8 people will be found, assigned to random positions. In order to overcome the experience, you must correctly select the person to look for.

Gesture recognition

To say hi, you have to move your hand 5 times and the person will do an error animation if you failed or a success animation if you were right.
